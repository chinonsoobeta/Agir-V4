# Permit research coverage

Agir exposes all 21 Metro Vancouver municipalities plus the City of Kelowna as
research jurisdictions. Coverage is deliberately labelled. A jurisdiction being
available in Agir does not mean every permit category has been reviewed or that a
candidate applies to a project.

## Coverage states

- `not_started`: the official municipal homepage is recorded; category-specific
  source review remains open.
- `partial`: one or more category-specific official sources are recorded; gaps
  remain visible as unreviewed.
- `reviewed`: every supported category has a current review record from an
  assigned qualified reviewer. No jurisdiction is set to this state by migration.

## Launch catalogue

Partial coverage: Burnaby, Coquitlam, New Westminster, Richmond, Surrey,
Vancouver, and Kelowna.

Coverage review not started: Anmore, Belcarra, Bowen Island, Delta, City of
Langley, Township of Langley, Lions Bay, Maple Ridge, City of North Vancouver,
District of North Vancouver, Pitt Meadows, Port Coquitlam, Port Moody, West
Vancouver, and White Rock.

## Review gate

For each municipality and permit category, record the official URL, source title,
verbatim supporting text, applicability limits, reviewer, review date, next-review
date, official-source status, and freshness status. Unavailable, contradictory,
or incomplete sources stay visible and never become automatic requirements.
